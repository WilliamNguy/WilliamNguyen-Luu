About Me

Hey! I’m William, a Canadian artist based in Montreal. I’m currently completing my undergraduate degree in the BFA Specialization in Computation Arts, where I explore the intersection of art and technology. My work focuses primarily on 3D modelling and animation using Blender, combining technical precision with a strong interest in visual storytelling and digital environments. I also have experience in creative coding and developed this website using JavaScript to showcase my work and document my evolving practice.
